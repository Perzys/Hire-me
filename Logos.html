My Logo

Designed with: Illustrator, Photoshop
Description: I designed this logo in 2017 because I wanted to create something that could be recognized as my brand. I knew I wanted to use my initials somehow, and after sketching for an hour or two, I was happiest with this minimalistic design. Because the design is not unnecessarily complicated, it can be easily recognized as my initials, and my brand.

Crowned

Designed with: Illustrator, Photoshop
Description: This was a graphic design I created for my church small group's shirt. The design is inspired by Revelation 4:10-11, where elders cast their crowns before the throne of God. The crowns are "cracked" to contrast between the inperfection of what man has to offer compared to the divine.
