ABOUT PORTFOLIO CONTACT

OVERVIEW

Role: UI/UX designer, Front end developer
Front End: HTML, CSS, Javascript
Back End: Node.js, Facebook Login API, Yelp API, Google Login API, Google Maps API
Conceptualization: Have you and your friends ever been so indecisive on where to eat that you ended up wasting 20 minutes doing nothing but standing in a circle looking at your phones? My teammates and I have, more times that I can count, which is what inspired us to make Birdie.
Description: Birdie is a mobile web app that uses AI technology to streamline the decision making process when trying to find a place to eat. Birdie learns what you do and don't like to determine which restaurants to recommend to you. You can either have Birdie decide for you, or quickly find a place to eat from a wide selection of cuisines.
Current Prototype: Birdie prototype (best viewed with mobile)
